Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can keep the original format or convert to another format during compression.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider resizing first.

How much can I reduce my image size?

Depending on the image and settings, you can typically reduce file size by 50-80% without noticeable quality loss.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
